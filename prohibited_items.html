Prohibited Items

Firearms and explosives are restricted items that cannot be taken into airports due to security concerns.

Liquids, gels, and aerosols exceeding the allowed volume limit are prohibited from being carried onto airplanes. Sharp objects such as knives, scissors, and razor blades are restricted items and must be packed in checked luggage rather than carried onto the aircraft.
